Professional PS Vita Services in South Florida

The PlayStation Vita (both the 1000 OLED model and the 2000 Slim model) remains a powerhouse of portable gaming. At DEY6 Console in Boca Raton, we specialize in breathing new life into these fantastic systems. Whether you are dealing with the dreaded analog stick drift or looking to unlock the full potential of your device with an SD2Vita adapter, our technical team has you covered.

Complete Vita Maintenance & Upgrades

  • Analog Stick Replacement: Stick drift can ruin your gaming experience. We replace faulty analog sticks with OEM-quality ALPS modules, restoring perfect deadzones and accuracy.
  • OLED & LCD Screen Replacements: The Vita 1000's OLED screen is gorgeous but susceptible to burn-in, while the 2000's LCD can suffer from physical damage. We replace front panels meticulously to ensure no dust intrusion.
  • SD2Vita Configuration: Ditch the overpriced proprietary memory cards. We provide hardware setup for SD2Vita adapters, allowing you to use high-capacity micro-SD cards for vastly expanded storage.
  • Battery Replacements: If your Vita dies quickly or shuts off randomly, the lithium-ion battery has degraded. We install fresh, tested batteries to restore factory battery life.

We pride ourselves on clean internal work, ensuring proper ribbon cable seating and thermal management during reassembly. Contact our Boca Raton office to schedule your drop-off.
