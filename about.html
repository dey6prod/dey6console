About DEY6 Console

Located in the heart of Boca Raton, Florida, DEY6 Console was founded on a passion for precision engineering and video game preservation. We understand that gaming consoles are complex pieces of specialized hardware that require meticulous care.

We serve the local South Florida gaming community with pride, offering transparent diagnostics, premium parts sourcing, and dedicated customer communication. Your hardware is an investment; let us help you maintain it.
