About DEY6 Console

Located in the heart of Boca Raton, Florida, DEY6 Console was founded on a passion for precision engineering and video game preservation. We understand that gaming consoles are complex pieces of specialized hardware that require more than just a quick fix—they require meticulous care, the right tools, and an understanding of their unique architecture.

Whether it's routing delicate flex ribbons through a Nintendo 3DS hinge, calibrating optical lasers on a PlayStation 2, or optimizing a PS Vita for modern use, we approach every project with technical rigor. We don't cut corners on thermal paste, we don't rush shell alignments, and we test every input before returning your device.

We serve the local South Florida gaming community with pride, offering transparent diagnostics, premium parts sourcing, and dedicated customer communication. Your hardware is an investment; let us help you maintain it.
