Comprehensive Nintendo 3DS Repair in Boca Raton

The Nintendo 3DS remains one of the most beloved handheld gaming platforms of all time. However, due to its clamshell design and age, hardware degradation is common. At DEY6 Console, based locally in Boca Raton, Florida, we specialize in the meticulous restoration and modification of the entire Nintendo 3DS family.

Our Core 3DS Services

  • Screen Replacement & IPS Upgrades: Top and bottom screen replacements and highly sought-after dual IPS screen calibrations.
  • Shell & Housing Replacement: From standard OEM colors to custom aftermarket housings.
  • Hinge Repair: We replace the central hinge barrel and carefully reroute your internal components.
  • Charging Port & Battery Maintenance: Rapid soldering repairs for the charging port and high-capacity replacement batteries.
